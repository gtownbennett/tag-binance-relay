TAG Prediction Ledger v2.4.0

What happens automatically:
1. Each time you tap Analyze TAG, Chad makes 6h, 24h, 3d and 7d forecasts.
2. The relay saves the starting price, leverage/spot setup and forecast ranges.
3. On later requests, forecasts whose due time passed are graded automatically.
4. After at least 8 horizons are graded, Chad receives the accuracy summary and may cautiously recalibrate confidence.

New protected endpoints in /docs:
- GET /v1/chad/ledger
- GET /v1/chad/performance
- POST /v1/chad/ledger/grade
- GET /v1/chad/ledger/export

You do not need to add or change any Render environment variable for the first test.
The default database is temporary on Render and can be erased by a restart or redeploy.
Use the export endpoint for backup until durable storage is added.
